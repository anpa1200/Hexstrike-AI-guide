How It Works

Four steps, one prompt

Write your objective. HexStrike does the rest.

1

Write a Goal

Describe your objective in plain language — "Scan this subnet and find exploitable services" or "Crack this PDF password."

2

HexStrike Plans

The LLM reasons about the goal, selects the right tools, sequences the attack chain, and handles dependencies automatically.

3

Tools Execute

HexStrike fires the tools, parses results, recovers from errors, and feeds findings back into the LLM for the next decision.

4

Report Delivered

Structured findings with evidence, CVSS scores, and remediation recommendations — ready to deliver.
